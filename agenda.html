Political Agenda

Putting People First - 5-Point Agenda

1

Cost of Living Relief

Implementing policies to reduce housing costs, support small businesses, and ease financial burden on working families.

2

Education & Skills Development

Investing in quality education, vocational training, and mentorship programs for all Australians.

3

Migration Reform

Streamlining immigration processes while maintaining strict security and ensuring fair treatment of newcomers.

4

Local Business Support

Creating opportunities for small business growth and supporting local entrepreneurs with resources and mentorship.

5

Youth & Women's Representation

Amplifying voices of youth and women in leadership, ensuring diverse perspectives shape policy decisions.
