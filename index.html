Councillor Founder Editor-in-Chief Liberal Candidate

Jugandeep Singh is a dedicated community leader, entrepreneur, and advocate for inclusive growth. From his roots in Punjab to serving on Blacktown City Council, his journey embodies resilience, vision, and an unwavering commitment to empowering every Australian. As a Liberal Party candidate, education pioneer, and media founder, Jugandeep champions policies that put people first—creating opportunities, celebrating diversity, and building a future where everyone belongs.

Leadership Across Multiple Spheres

Councillor, Blacktown City Council
